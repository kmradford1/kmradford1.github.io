## Client
Shift Ease is an app developed for restaurant and service industry employers and employees. 

## Team
Individual

## Project Description
Shift Ease makes it easier to view, alter, and switch schedules. It also enables staff to easily switch shifts for approval as well as add things to a calendar for all employees and employers to view.
