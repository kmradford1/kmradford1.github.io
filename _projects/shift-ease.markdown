## Team

## Project Description
Shift Ease makes it easier to view, alter, and switch schedules. It also enables staff to easily switch shifts for approval as well as add things to a calendar for all employees and employers to view.![Screen Shot 2019-01-31 at 2.49.23 PM.png](/uploads/Screen%20Shot%202019-01-31%20at%202.49.23%20PM.png)![Screen Shot 2019-01-31 at 2.49.03 PM.png](/uploads/Screen%20Shot%202019-01-31%20at%202.49.03%20PM.png)
